Research Gap

Many researchers have explored the possibility of using Machine Learning to overcome the challenges of understanding and processing large volumes of documents in human languages to accomplish very specific goals. There have been studies on keyword extraction, key phrase extraction, document clustering and text summarization. However, there has not been a strong focus on the application of such techniques on news which is a unique data set and the value to be gained by doing so. Google News and Monok.com are the prominent existing tools that use machine learning to process news. However, none of them are open source and the underlying technology has not been disclosed. The set of features offered is also very limited and none of them work with Sri Lankan news services.

Research Problem

There are many news services in Sri Lanka that publish online content to their vast numbers of followers. Most often, reports from different sources on the same incident differ both in quantity and quality of information. However, most of the public follow only a very limited number of sources that they are used to. This is undesirable for multiple reasons. People lose access to key pieces of information that the sources they follow did not pick up and different perspectives on the same event. This is mainly due to the absence of a good news aggregator tailored for Sri Lanka that facilitates reading news about the same event from multiple sources on a single location.

Research Objectives

The main objective of the proposed research is to build an automated news aggregation platform tailored to Sri Lanka that would enable the public to efficiently consume news from multiple sources through all available viewpoints and one that would raise awareness on ethical journalism.

Specific Objectives:
Categorize news articles into pre-defined categories.
Group news articles that are referring to the same incident. 
Tool for the journalists to pre-validate their own work.  
Flag news articles that violate media ethics. 
Summarize lengthy news articles.




Methodology

Creating a machine learning model to group news articles referring to the same incident.

Creating a classifier model to classify news into predefined categories.

Creating a machine learning model to identify news articles that violate media ethics.

Creating a machine learning model to summarize news articles.

Creating a machine learning model to generate tags for a news article.
